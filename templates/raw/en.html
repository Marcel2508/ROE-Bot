RoE Project:
  • Is called "miner" a survey ship who are mining any node
  • Is called "empty" any ship with no resources to steal by destroying it
  • Is called "overcargo" any ship that after scanning has resource onboard
  • Is called "zero node" a mining node with no resources left to gather


RoE for EU-164 (incomplete, these are the rules most people agree on):
  1. Don't attack empty miners
  2. Overcargos can be attacked at all times
  3. You can attack miners sitting on zero node after a warning (5min min.)
  4. Warships can be attacked at all times


Notes:
  • Alliances renforce the terms between concerned alliances/players (don't attack your allies)
  • Wars cancel the terms between concerned alliances/players (no RoE when in war)
